Mars has polar ice caps of water and dry ice. It is thought that Mars once had very large rivers of water but most of it was lost to space due to low gravity and lack of a protective atmosphere. Measurements indicate that Mars continues to leave a trail of atmospheric elements behind itself on its orbit; meaning it is still losing its atmosphere.
